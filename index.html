Free vs Plus

Start free. Upgrade only when DoorCodes grows with you.

DoorCodes is designed to be useful before you pay. DoorCodes Plus is a one-time unlock for people who need more saved places or private iCloud sync across their own Apple devices.

Free

  • Up to 3 saved places
  • Secure Reveal with Face ID, Touch ID, or passcode
  • Nearby, Recents, manual access, and privacy-safe reminders
  • Local-only storage if you prefer to keep everything on one device

DoorCodes Plus

  • Unlimited saved places
  • Private iCloud sync across your own iPhone and iPad
  • Restore Purchase from Settings whenever needed
  • One-time purchase — no subscription
