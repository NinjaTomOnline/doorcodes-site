# Doorcodes One-Sheet

## One-Line Pitch

Doorcodes keeps the right access code ready when you arrive, without putting raw codes on your lock screen.

## What It Is

Doorcodes is an iPhone utility for people who need gate, garage, storage, building, and lockbox codes at specific places. Users save a place, attach one or more access codes, and reveal the right detail when they need to manually enter it.

## Who It Helps

- Apartment and condo residents with gate, garage, lobby, package room, and amenity codes.
- Storage users who forget gate or unit details between visits.
- Realtors and property managers juggling showing-day gate, keybox, parking, and front-door notes.
- Short-term rental hosts, cleaners, pet sitters, and service providers who return to many addresses.

## Product Boundary

Doorcodes does not remotely unlock anything. It helps users organize and reveal codes they already have so they can type them into a keypad, gate panel, storage entry, building door, or realtor keybox.

## Privacy Angle

Doorcodes is designed around practical privacy. Arrival reminders can give context without exposing raw access codes on the lock screen, and sensitive codes can be protected with Face ID or Touch ID reveal.

## Campaign Line

Save it once. Find it when you arrive.
