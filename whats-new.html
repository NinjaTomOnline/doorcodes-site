Highlights

What stands out in DoorCodes 1.0

These are the biggest customer-facing ideas in the first release.

Access details by place

DoorCodes keeps recurring entries organized by the real places where they matter instead of forcing you to search old notes and messages.

Private by default

Reminders are designed to be useful without exposing raw access codes on the lock screen, and Secure Reveal helps protect sensitive details inside the app.

Optional private sync

If you want your places and settings to follow your own Apple devices, DoorCodes can sync through your private iCloud account.
