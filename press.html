Story Angles

Coverage hooks

These are the safest launch angles for coverage and outreach.

Everyday utility

DoorCodes solves the small but familiar moment of arriving at a gate, garage, building, or office and searching old messages for the right code.

Privacy-first organization

DoorCodes gives users a private place for recurring access details, with privacy-safe reminders and Secure Reveal for sensitive codes.

For renters and storage users

DoorCodes helps keep apartment, lobby, package room, storage gate, and unit details attached to the place where they matter.

For realtors and service visits

DoorCodes can keep showing-day gate, keybox, parking, and entry details organized without positioning the app as a remote unlock tool.

Messaging Note

Please avoid remote-unlock language.

DoorCodes does not open locks, doors, gates, garages, or keyboxes. It stores and reveals access codes users already have so they can manually enter them into the appropriate keypad or entry system.
